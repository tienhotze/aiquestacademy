Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our 4-week introductory AI Quest program.

Does my child need any coding or prior AI experience?

Absolutely not! This is an **introductory** course designed for beginners aged 10-17. We focus on understanding and using AI tools creatively, not complex coding. Curiosity is the only prerequisite!

What ages is this program for?

This introductory program is specifically designed for two age groups: 10-13 years old (Innovators) and 14-17 years old (Pioneers). Activities are tailored accordingly.

How long is the course?

This is a 4-week introductory course. Sessions are held once per week, consisting of 2 hours of AI learning followed by 1 hour of trampoline park access.

Should my child bring their own laptop?

Children are welcome to bring their own laptops (Windows or Mac) with Wi-Fi capability. We also design activities so that two children can comfortably share one laptop if needed. Please indicate your preference when registering interest.

What AI tools will be used? Are they safe?

We primarily use widely available, web-based AI tools often with free tiers, such as Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot (including its image creator), Perplexity AI, and tools within Canva. We prioritize tools with appropriate content filters and always supervise usage. We teach critical evaluation and safe interaction.

What are the class sizes?

We keep class sizes small to ensure personalized attention and effective group collaboration. Typically, classes range from 8 to 12 students per instructor.

Is the trampoline park access supervised?

Our instructors oversee the transition, but the trampoline park operates under its own safety rules, waivers, and staffing. Parents should review the park's specific policies. Access is a bonus activity after the formal AI instruction concludes.

What if my child misses one of the 4 sessions?

We understand occasional absences happen. While missed sessions cannot be rescheduled, we can provide brief summaries or materials covered upon request. Consistent attendance over the 4 weeks is encouraged for the best learning experience.
